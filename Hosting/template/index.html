I am a dedicated researcher currently serving as a Research Assistant in the Economics Department at Kathmandu University School of Arts, under the guidance of Dr. Santosh Adhikari. Alongside, I collaborate as an independent researcher with Samriddhi Foundation, focusing primarily on policy research and policy briefs centered on Madesh Province.


My research interests lie in the field of econometrics, with a particular emphasis on causal inference. Over the years, I have worked on a diverse range of topics, including labor economics, institutional economics, and environmental economics.


Previously, I served as a Policy Research Fellow at Samriddhi Foundation and earned my Bachelor’s degree in Economics from Kathmandu University in 2024. Through this platform, I aim to share my ongoing projects, insights, and contributions to the field of economics.
